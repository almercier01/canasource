CanaSource Brand Guidelines

Logo Colors:
- Red: #d62828
- Black: #111827
- Gray: #6b7280
- Accent: #4caf50 (green)

Typography:
- Headings: Inter Bold
- Body: Inter Regular

Logo Usage:
- Do not stretch or modify the logo colors.
- Maintain padding of 16px around the logo.
- When on dark backgrounds, use white or the inverted logo.

Favicon:
Included in /favicon folder for browser and app use.

If you need scalable vector formats or monochrome variations, contact press@canasource.ca
